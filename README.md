## Car Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This repository contains the files and documentation for a comprehensive Power BI dashboard project designed to analyze and visualize crucial car sales data. The dashboard provides strategic and operational insights by tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) and identifying trends across various dimensions (time, geography, and car attributes).

### **Features**

* **Time-Intelligence KPIs:** Real-time metrics for Year-to-Date (YTD) and Month-to-Date (MTD) Total Sales, Cars Sold, and Average Price, complete with **Year-over-Year (YOY) growth** and absolute difference comparisons.
* **Sales Trend Analysis:** A line chart visualizing **YTD Sales Weekly Trend** to identify peak selling seasons and monitor sales velocity.
* **Market Segmentation:** Donut charts detailing the contribution of different **Body Styles** and **Colors** to the overall YTD Total Sales revenue.
* **Geographic Insights:** An interactive Map visualization showcasing **YTD Cars Sold by Dealer Region** to pinpoint top-performing and underperforming territories.
* **Company Performance:** A detailed tabular grid providing a company-wise sales breakdown, including YTD Total Sales, Cars Sold, and Average Price.
* **Interactive Filtering:** Dedicated slicers for filtering data by **Body Style, Dealer Name, Transmission, and Engine** for granular analysis.

### **Business Problem Solved**

The dashboard addresses two key challenges:

1.  **KPI Requirement:** Establishing a single source of truth for all critical sales metrics to enable stakeholders to quickly assess business health and track progress against targets.
2.  **Visualization Requirement:** Transforming raw transactional data into actionable visualizations that highlight **where, when, and what** is selling best, supporting decisions in inventory management, marketing spend allocation, and regional strategy.
